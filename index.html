About Hakka Noddle

Hakka Noddles is popular Indo-chinese dish of stir fired noddles,veggies and sauces.It is hugely popular in indian resturant and stalls.

Ingredients:

  • noodles
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp chili garlic sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
Prep Times
5min
Cook Time
20min
Total Time
25min

Instructions:

  1. Cook the noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, and chili garlic sauce.
  3. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant.
  4. Add the cooked noodles to the pan and pour the sauce mixture over them. Toss well to coat the noodles evenly.
  5. Stir in the chopped green onions and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  6. Remove from heat and serve hot.
